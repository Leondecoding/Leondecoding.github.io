Inside the Studio

FZ Studio is a small, quiet workspace in London. This is where I spend most days with watercolour, watching how light moves across paper and everyday objects. I don’t appear on camera very often, but every painting you see online begins here – on this table by the window.

What I’m working on now

My work moves between two kinds of paintings:

– realistic observation studies of flowers and simple objects, where I practise seeing light and colour clearly
– Inner Light paintings, softer and more poetic scenes inspired by mornings, forest edges and small details from daily life

The studies often appear in my “paint with me” videos. The Inner Light works become the originals, prints and greeting cards on this website.

Observation Studies

Before I enter the Inner Light series, I make many small realism studies. They help me slow down, notice how colours blend, and let my hand find a calm rhythm.

These works are not for sale – they are simply notes from my daily practice. You may recognise some of them from my YouTube channel.
